Methodology & integrity note. No failures were deleted or hidden. Every test case from the CI run is represented with its actual verdict. Failures are classified by root cause: Automation defect = the test harness could not drive the app (locator/navigation drift against the current SwiftUI build, or a feature not exposed in this build); Product (review) = the failure may reflect real app behaviour and is escalated, not dismissed; Env / under review = environment (e.g. auth-token expiry over a multi-hour run) or a new check still being validated. The "product-signal pass" rate = passed ÷ (passed + product + under-review failures). Automation defects flagged as "fixed in remediation branch" have code fixes committed; a confirmation re-run is in progress.
